About Us

Amateur of films or Series, you are on the good site! Easy to use and allows you to share the films you love with all your friends and family. After having created an account, all the doors open. You will be able to add the movies or tv shows you prefer and to visualize all the films and series added by the other users. First of all it gives you good ideas of films and series if you are in search of some. Secondly, you have the possibility to vote for those you already saw and that you have appreciated! A ranking is done according to the number of votes, then if you want to view the trendy movies of the month, look at our top ten.

How to use the website

To be able to access the real part of the website, you will have to login. To do so just click on the login button. If you are not a member yet, click on "sign up". You will then be redirected to the pages of Tv shows and movies. You can vote for those you like and if it isn't already in the list, you can add it by going to the page "Add Tvshow/Movie".
